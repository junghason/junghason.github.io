About

I hold a Ph.D. in AI & Big Data, and my work sits at the intersection of data science, statistics, and machine learning. My recent interests center on large language models (LLMs) and AI Transformation (AX) — understanding how modern AI systems can be rigorously evaluated and meaningfully integrated into real-world products, organizations, and decision-making.

Research Interests

  • Data Science & Statistics — statistical modeling, experimental design, and drawing reliable conclusions from large-scale data.
  • Machine Learning — predictive modeling and applied ML systems.
  • Large Language Models — applications, evaluation, and LLM-powered workflows.
  • AI Transformation (AX) — adopting AI across products and organizations.
